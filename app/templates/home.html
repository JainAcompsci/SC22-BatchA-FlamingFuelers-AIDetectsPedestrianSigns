Safety for Blind Pedestrians.
An AI project Made by High School Students.

About Our Model

Our AI model is designed to assist those who are visually impaired. Since people who are visually impaired cannot use the assistance of braille or their walking sticks to read pedestrian road signs, they may be at risk of serious accidents when crossing the street and other issues that could jeopardize their safety. To combat these safety hazards, we decided to create an AI model that detects common road and pedestrian signs and reads the signs detected aloud to the user in an audio message. We used computer vision packages, APIs, and tools, including SerpAPI, YOLOv5, and Roboflow, to put together our project. We would like to thank AI camp for teaching us about the necessary concepts and our instructor who guided us through each and every step of the process.
